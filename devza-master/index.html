Meet the Owner

Hello,
my name is Ryan. I am the lead designer, consultant, and owner of Devza Professional Services. I have been designing for the past five years now, and have worked on projects from banner design to full-scale web design. Over the years I have developed my philosophy on what design is, and also what it should be. During this time, I have seen my fair share of gross gradients, shiny buttons, and hard-to-navigate UI/UX designs.

In my free time, I enjoy to road trip, taking photos of nature and my surroundings. I enjoy cinematography and photography as a full-time hobby.

American Sentry Handyman Services

Logo Design

The owner of American Sentry started out with a simple image logo done in Photoshop. He mentioned his lack of experience in Photoshop beforehand, and explained how he wanted to use simple shapes in his original design. I took this into account when designing the new logo, as I knew that he would want it printed on tees, hats, and included on his website. I started by sketching and mocking up multiple designs with differential colour schemes. The client ended up liking the red and tan colour scheme from another design, and the shape shown in the final design. Overall, the finished product turned out professional looking and met the clients needs.
