Our Minimal Design Process

Our process allows a nailed-down design to be created faster. We stick to a strict schedule to get your prject completed on time. Check in on us every step of the way with our intuitive client portal.

Consult

We woork with you to decide what your needs and wants are, and how we can meet them. Taking notes and sketches allows us to save every intricut detail for planning.

Plan

A skeletal structure of your site is built during the planning phase.

Build

This is when all of your ideas come to life. Based on the plans, a rough draft of a design is put together. This includes colors, layouts, and later the bulk of the content.

Revise

Taking it back to the drawing board, we refine your design to your exact specifications. Content, images, and colors can be changed to achieve the final desired effect.
