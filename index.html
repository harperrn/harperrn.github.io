About Us


Simple, well designed webpages. That is what we think the web should be. How many times have you been looking on a site for information, and just can't find it because the page is so badly designed? Simple web navigation is easy to create but is overlooked in many web projects. We specialize in simple, informational company and business webpages and graphic design. In our web projects, nothing is overlooked. Our clients choose everything from the images to the colors and fonts that are used. Simple web design should be just that, simple.

Design Process

First, we get as much information as we can from our clients. We inquire about ideas, design style, needs, wants, etc. From there, we build a comprehensive business plan including deployment date, what we need from our client, pricing, and what we provide for our client as far as the site and design go. If there are any other requests, they will need to be added to a revised version of this plan before a contract is erected. After the contract is finished and signed, work on the site is started.
